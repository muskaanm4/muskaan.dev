About Me


I’m Muskaan, and I write code for a living, for school, and occasionally in my sleep when a bug decides to haunt my dreams. Currently, I am splitting my sanity between studying at UNASAT and working as an actual Software Engineer at Assuria N.V.

Meaning I'm equally comfortable panicking over an error at 2 AM.

In 2023, purely for the plot (and a bit of fun), I founded 'Get IT Done' - a mini-startup that is somehow still alive and kicking. We specialize in building responsive websites and clean flyer designs.

From working on deep functional applications to fighting the laws of physics to center pizza icons on an Italian food website, I love the absolute rush of solving problems. I approach engineering with a firm belief that 90% of computer science is just typing your password blindly into a terminal while pretending you know exactly what’s about to happen.
